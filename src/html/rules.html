TES Rules

  1. Be respectful to all Titan E-sport’s members, staff, and admin
  2. Any mentions of threatening, violating, harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks against Titan E-sport’s members or their family will result in an immediate and permanent ban
  3. Sending/linking any harmful material (i.e. viruses, malware, IP grabbers, harmware, etc.) will result in an immediate and permanent ban
  4. Posting personal information, photos, or video of Titan E-sport’s members, staff, or admin without their permission will result in an immediate and permanent ban
  5. Sending/linking any graphical material (i.e. things NSFW - pictures of minors, violent acts, gruesome images, etc.) will result in an immediate and permanent ban
  6. Excessive spam – that includes needless pinging (e.g. @everyone, @admin, @staff, etc.) without proper reasoning, loud/obnoxious noises in voice channels, image/message spam, etc. - can result in a temporary ban of your privileges on Titan E-sport’s server
  7. Post content in the proper channels. If you excessively post material in the wrong channels, this can result in a temporary ban of your privileges on Titan E-sport’s server
  8. All members of Titan E-sports are ambassadors for this organization. Violating the rules of sportsmanship (i.e. cheating, boosting, account sharing, smurfing, feeding, obnoxious behavior in game, etc.) will not be tolerated and can result in a temporary ban of your privileges on Titan E-sport’s server
  9. Advertising that is not permitted by staff or admin will result in a temporary ban of your privileges on Titan E-sport’s server
  10. Staff and admin have final say in all resolutions. If you have an issue with a Titan E-sport’s member, please use the proper channels to report them. First step is to fill out a report form (link below) then inform an admin or staff member
